Andrew Kwong

I am a second year Ph.D. student studying computer science at the University of Michigan. My advisor is Kevin Fu. I received my BA in Mathematics and BS in Computer Science from UCSC in 2016, where I worked with the SSRC on improving the performance of galois field arithmetic in the context of erasure codes. When I was an undergrad, I ran a security club that focused on lock picking and competing in CTF hacking competitions. I still compete from time to time with Michigan's CTF team, Wolverines4Kegan.

Research Interests

  • Embedded Security
  • Side Channels
  • Applied Cryptography
  • Malware Analysis
